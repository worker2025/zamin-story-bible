# Film Bible: Location Continuity

## Rule
Locations must appear only in the states documented at each point in the
Story Bible's timeline. Do not show a location's later state (e.g.,
Zapol-e-Simi emptied of its retinue) in an earlier scene, or vice versa.

## Key Location State Changes

- Kolfarah relief: shown "being carved" in Chapter 1 (in-progress state);
  no later completed-state description is given in the Story Bible beyond
  the Appendix's art-historical description, which is presented as the
  finished, historically-documented relief, not a specific later story
  scene.
- Zapol-e-Simi: populated with commanders, priests, scribes through
  Chapter 9's opening; explicitly described as emptied ("جایی که روزی صفِ
  فرماندهان، کاهنان و دبیران تا پلهها میرسید، اکنون خالی بود") by the end
  of Chapter 9, after Hani's departure and the staff's dispersal. Any scene
  set at Zapol-e-Simi after Chapter 9 must reflect this emptied state; the
  Story Bible does not depict any scene there after Chapter 9.
- Shileyti: site of the raid/battle (Chapter 7) -> mourning and slow
  rebuilding (Chapters 7-9) -> not the primary setting after Chapter 9 in
  the Story Bible's documented events (subsequent councils move to
  Khong-e-Azhdar and then Eshkaft-e-Salman).
- Khong-e-Azhdar: site of the first large popular assembly (Chapters
  10-11); the council process is explicitly stated to move to
  Eshkaft-e-Salman starting Chapter 12 ("فردا، راهِ اشکفتسلمان را پیش
  میگرفتند").
