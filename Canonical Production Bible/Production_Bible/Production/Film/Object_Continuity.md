# Film Bible: Object Continuity

## Rule
Object continuity must follow the ownership chain documented in V05_Objects.md
and the corresponding Objects/ sheets. Do not show an object in a scene
before its first documented appearance or after it is documented as lost,
destroyed, or transferred, without following the documented transfer chain.

## Key Object Chains

### Chang (چنگ)
Nika (Ch.1-7) -> Manzat (per dying instruction, Ch.7) -> co-custody with Ani
(Ch.7 onward) -> Ani plays it publicly first (Ch.9) -> unnamed girl plays it
at Eshkaft-e-Salman (Ch.12), after which it is called "چنگِ میدان" rather
than "چنگِ نیکا." (Source: Objects/Chang.md)

### Oak-leaf boat
Made and re-made repeatedly by Manzat from Chapter 1 onward; becomes a
copied motif among other children (Chapter 5); final appearance floated
beside Kuru's memorial brick at the novel's end (Chapter 13). (Source:
Objects/Oak-leaf_Boat.md)

### Broken brick / Nika's grave marker
Picked up by Nika at Kolfarah (Chapter 1) -> kept in her bundle -> used,
inscribed with a boat/water/sun, as her grave marker at Shileyti (Chapter 7).
(Source: Objects/Broken_Brick.md)

### Kuru's sword
Worn by Kuru (Chapter 5 onward) -> thrown down before Hani in protest
(Chapter 8) -> returned to Kuru by Hani (Chapter 8) -> presumed worn again
afterward (not separately re-described). (Source: Characters/Kuru.md)

### Treasury seal
Held by Nisiker -> placed on the stone and relinquished at his departure
(Chapter 9). Subsequent handling: UNKNOWN. (Source: Objects sheets,
V03_Events.md E45)

### Vote-bricks
Introduced at Khong-e-Azhdar (Chapter 10) -> formalized at the shura
(Chapter 11) -> carried by caravan to other villages (Chapters 12-13).
(Source: Objects/Vote-Bricks.md)
