# Film Bible: Timeline Continuity

## Rule
No absolute calendar dates exist in the Story Bible. Continuity must be
maintained using only the relative time-markers documented in
V06_Timeline.md. Do not assign specific dates, seasons beyond what is
stated, or fill time gaps with invented detail.

## Key Continuity Checkpoints (from V06_Timeline.md)

1. Chapter 1 begins on an autumn, misty morning, before sunrise.
2. Chapters 1-7 (Nika's death) elapse over an unspecified number of days;
   only chapter-internal relative markers exist — no cumulative total is
   stated in the Story Bible.
3. Chapter 8 opens at +5 days after Nika's death.
4. The ceasefire is declared within Chapter 8, an unspecified number of
   days after it opens (bounded only by the "+5 days" marker at the
   chapter's start and "یک روز" for the messenger's arrival within it).
5. Chapter 9 opens at +3 weeks after the ceasefire, and elapses a further
   "چند روز" (a few days) then "دو هفتهی دیگر" (another two weeks)
   internally.
6. Chapter 10 opens at +3 winters after the ceasefire.
7. Chapters 11-13 follow in quick, explicitly stated succession: same
   evening, next day, three mornings later, departure day — with no large
   time-jumps.

## Prohibited Continuity Assumptions
- Do not assume any specific month, season name (beyond "autumn" for
  Chapter 1 and "winter" for the three-winters marker), or regnal/calendar
  year.
- Do not assume the exact number of days between Nika's death and the
  ceasefire beyond the bounds given above — this interval is UNKNOWN
  precisely.

## Validation
- No invented information
- Derived only from Story Bible
- UNKNOWN preserved
- Canon preserved
