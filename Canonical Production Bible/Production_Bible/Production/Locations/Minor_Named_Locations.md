# Location Sheet: Minor Named Locations

## Overview
This composite sheet lists all locations named in the Story Bible (V04_Locations.md, "Other named places") that are mentioned in dialogue, footnotes, or as origin-villages of assembly attendees, but for which the Story Bible records no further descriptive detail (architecture, environment, lighting, or sound). Per Zero Invention Policy, no such detail is added here. Each entry lists only the Story Bible's stated context of first mention.
