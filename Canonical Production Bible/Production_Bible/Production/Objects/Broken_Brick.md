# Object Sheet: Broken Brick

## Owner
Nika (found by her; later serves as her grave marker). (Source: V05_Objects.md)

## Material
Fired/unfired clay brick (خشت), broken, half missing, edges worn ("نیمهاش گم شده و لبههایش خُرد شده بود"). (Source: V05_Objects.md)

## Appearances
- Chapter 1 — Nika picks it up near a stonecutter at Kolfarah, keeps it in her bundle (بقچه). (V05_Objects.md)
- Chapter 7 — used as her grave marker at Shileyti, inscribed by her with a boat, water, and sun. (V05_Objects.md)

## Events
E05 (first appearance, within Kolfarah scene), E29 (burial). (Source: V03_Events.md)

## Meaning (only as explicitly stated in Story Bible)
- No headstone is used; only this marked broken brick. (Source: V05_Objects.md, V07_Culture.md)
- Nika's grandmother's remembered saying, associated with stone generally: "سنگ، آنچه را به او سپرده شود، فراموش نمیکند." (Source: V02_Characters.md)
