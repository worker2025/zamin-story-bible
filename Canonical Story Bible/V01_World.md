- Evidence — Chapter: پیوست — Quote: "هانی، هوهین و شوترورو شخصیتهایی با پشتوانهی تاریخیاند و آیاپیر، کولفرح، خونْگْاَژدر و اشکفتسلمان مکانهایی واقعی هستند. در مواردی نیز برای سهولتِ شناساییِ جغرافیای روایت، از نامهای شناختهشدهی امروزینِ برخی مکانها و نواحی استفاده شده است."

**ARCHIVIST NOTE (not a manuscript fact, procedural only):** Per the author's own note above, only Hani, Hohin (Hoohin), and Shoterouro are stated to have historical grounding; Ayapir, Kolfarah, Khong-e-Azhdar, and Eshkaft-e-Salman are stated to be real places. All other characters, dialogue, rituals, and events are explicitly literary invention by the author — this bible still records them as canon of the NOVEL (in-fiction fact), not as external history.

## FACT: Setting — Geography (general)

The action moves between: کُهباد (Kohbad, a village), کولْفرح (Kolfarah, a shrine/temple precinct), زاپوالسیمی (Zapol-e-Simi, Hani's stone palace), آیاپیر (Ayapir, a market town), گُلِشور (Golshor, a caravanserai settlement in Zagros), شیلهیتی (Shileyti, a mountain region/village), خونْگْاَژدر (Khong-e-Azhdar, a rock-relief site), and اِشکفتسلمان (Eshkaft-e-Salman, a shrine site). Full location entries with evidence are catalogued in V04_Locations.
