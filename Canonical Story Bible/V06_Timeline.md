relative time-elapsed markers, which are recorded below in narrative order.

## Summary of Total Elapsed Story-Time (explicitly stated intervals only)

- Chapter 1 to Chapter 7 (Nika's death): elapses over an unspecified number of days/weeks — no cumulative total given; only chapter-internal markers exist (see above).
- Chapter 8 opens at **+5 days** after Nika's death.
- Chapter 9 opens at **+3 weeks** after the ceasefire (which itself occurs partway through Ch.8, an unspecified number of days after Nika's death — likely within the "five days" or shortly after, but the text does not give the exact day of the ceasefire relative to Nika's death beyond "پنج روز از مرگ نیکا گذشته بود" opening Ch.8, with the ceasefire messenger arriving "یک روز" later within that chapter).
- Chapter 9 additionally elapses **"چند روز"** + **"دو هفتهی دیگر"** internally.
- Chapter 10 opens at **+3 winters** after the ceasefire.
- Chapters 11–13 follow in quick, explicitly stated succession (same evening → next day → three mornings later → departure day) with no additional large time-jumps.
