# Location Sheet: Eshkaft-e-Salman

## Overview
A shrine/rock-relief site dedicated to Tarisha (تاریشا), reached about "دو سه ساعتی" (two to three hours) of travel from surrounding villages; becomes the final and larger seat of the popular council. Stated by the Story Bible to be a real, historically attested site. (Source: V04_Locations.md, V01_World.md, V09_Religion.md)

## Appendix Data (as printed in Story Bible)
- Four described reliefs: (1) Hani beside Hohin, children, and Shoterouro; (2) Hani with family, without the vizier; (3) Hani alone, with an extensive inscription; (4) an important Elamite figure performing a rite.
- Relief 3 inscription (partial): "«من، هانی، پیکر خود و هوهین، زن و خواهر محبوبم، و فرزندم را برای مادر تاریشا نصب کردم»..."
- Hohin's presence beside Hani in this relief is stated to be "حضوری کمنظیر در هنر و سنگنگاری جهان باستان" (a rare occurrence in ancient world art/relief-carving).
