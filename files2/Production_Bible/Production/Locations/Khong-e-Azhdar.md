## Appendix Data (as printed in Story Bible)
- Contains two rock reliefs on one slab: an Elamite-period relief (largely worn) reportedly depicting a public audience/gathering before the king — described as one of the oldest known depictions of "جمع شدنِ مردم"; and a Parthian-era relief showing Mehrdad II on horseback with two doves bearing a ring of power, granting legitimacy to a local ruler.
- Site used as open-air shelter "از ایالمِ میانه تا دوران اشکانی."
