# Location Sheet: Kolfarah

## Overview
Site of the temple of Narsina (نارسینا), with stone reliefs being carved of Hani's military victory; a cave used as a holding cell is located below/near the temple. Stated by the Story Bible to be a real, historically attested site. (Source: V04_Locations.md, V01_World.md)

## Appendix Data (Historical/Epigraphic — as printed in Story Bible)
- Main relief: Hani (round Elamite cap) beside Shoterouro; lower register shows cattle/goat sacrifice.
- Inscription: "«من، هانی، ۲۰ تن از سرکردگان شورشیان شیلهیتی را به اسارت درآوردم و خونشان را به کفاره ریختم. ۱۲۰ بز کوهی گرفتم و خونشان را در معبد قربانی کردم»."
- Second relief ("نقشبرجسته چهارسو"): four men carrying a god's statue, with unnamed captives in the border.
- Author's note: the anonymous captives in this relief's border were a direct inspiration for the novel's central theme.
(Source: V04_Locations.md, V01_World.md)
