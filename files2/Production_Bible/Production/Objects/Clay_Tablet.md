# Object Sheet: Clay Tablet

## Owner
Multiple: Shoterouro (official records, journal), Hohin (private records), Hani (father's old tablet, Hohin's tablet, Nisiker's tax tablets), Hotran (debtor records), unnamed scribes. (Source: V05_Objects.md)

## Material
Clay (گِل) — inscribed by pressing with a stylus/fingertip into soft clay ("با نوک انگشت، روی گِلِ نرم مینوشت"). (Source: V05_Objects.md, V03_Events.md E08)

## Appearances
- Chapter 2 — the Shileyti death-tally tablet Hani breaks. (V05_Objects.md)
- Chapter 4 — Hani's father's tablet on the Golshor closure; Hohin's tablet on the widow in the rain. (V05_Objects.md)
- Chapter 5 — Shoterouro's public proclamation tablet at Mozram spring. (V05_Objects.md)
- Chapter 9 — three tax-default tablets Nisiker presents to Hani. (V05_Objects.md)
- Chapters 8, 9 — Shoterouro's personal journal entries (quoted in full in Story Bible). (V05_Objects.md)

## Events
E07, E19, E20, E21, E38, E40, E42, E47. (Source: V03_Events.md)

## Meaning (only as explicitly stated in Story Bible)
- No general symbolic meaning is stated beyond its function as the medium of record-keeping; this evolves narratively into the "vote-brick" (خشت رأی) system — see Objects/Vote-Bricks.md. (Source: V05_Objects.md, V07_Culture.md)
