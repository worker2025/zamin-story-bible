# Visual Bible: Animals

## Stated Animal References

- Goat — a barefoot child is chasing a goat when Mamuru gives the child bread (Chapter 1). Hani later sits beside a goat in Kourash's cart when departing the palace (Chapter 9). (V02_Characters.md, V03_Events.md E46)
- Oxen — plowing near Kohbad with a wooden plow (gaavahan-e choobi), general village scene. (V04_Locations.md context)
- Mules — owned by Kourash; he addresses one directly in dialogue. (V02_Characters.md)
- Sheep — present at Kolfarah's work area. (V04_Locations.md)
- War-horse — tended by an old groom at Zapol-e-Simi's stable. (V04_Locations.md)
- Cow — Hohin is kicked by a cow at Khong-e-Azhdar. (V02_Characters.md)
- Buffalo — present at the Ayapir marsh area (هَوْرِه). (V04_Locations.md); buffalo hide used for Chishpish's and Ata's displays (V05_Objects.md)
- Dog — an ear-cropped dog ("سگِ گوشبریدهای") picked up by Kourash and jokingly placed on the council table. (V02_Characters.md)
- Mountain goats (بز کوهی) — referenced in the Kolfarah relief inscription: "۱۲۰ بز کوهی گرفتم و خونشان را در معبد قربانی کردم." (V04_Locations.md, Appendix data)
- Falcon (شاهین) — depicted image on Chishpish's hide, described as "god of the Persians," "guardian of covenants." (V02_Characters.md, V09_Religion.md)
- Bats — a cave used for hiding during the pursuit is described as smelling of bats. (V03_Events.md E23)
