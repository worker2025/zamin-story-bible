# V03 — Events

Numbering is sequential by first appearance in the manuscript (E01, E02...),
which also corresponds to narrative/chronological order as the novel is told
linearly with only a few internal-memory flashbacks (marked as such).

---

### E01 — Ordinary morning at Kohbad
**Chapter:** 1
**Scene:** Village of Kohbad at dawn; Mamuru bakes bread and gives some to a barefoot child; a lone rider is seen approaching from the court of Shotur-Nahunte.
**Evidence — Quote:** "در آن روزِ مهآلود پاییزی، کُهباد پیش از برآمدنِ آفتاب نفس میکشید."

### E02 — Tela reports increased levy to Nika
**Chapter:** 1
**Scene:** Nika wakes; Tela tells her the estate steward increased the wool-thread and fine-oil levy on three households (Nika's, Dolu Kodar's, and Naneh Rako's) for the second time this year.
**Evidence — Quote:** "دیشب باز مباشر اومد... خراجِ پشموخُمه و خیگِ روغنِ خوش رو بیشتر کرد..."

### E03 — Nika writes a protective inscription on the wall
**Chapter:** 1
**Evidence — Quote:** "«این سه خانه را کسی فراموش نکند»."

### E04 — Hani's morning at Zapol-e-Simi; childhood memory of a punished farmer
**Chapter:** 1
**Scene:** Hani wakes troubled; extinguishes a lantern; recalls a childhood ritual scene and his father imprisoning a farmer for late tax; the farmer disappears by morning.
**Evidence — Quote:** "آن شب، هانی صدای گریه زن دهقان را از پشت دیوار شنید... صبح که شد، دهقان را دیگر ندید."

### E05 — Nika walks to the temple of Narsina at Kolfarah; meets stonecutters and the boy Manzat
**Chapter:** 1
**Scene:** Nika passes through Kohbad, sees the rock reliefs of Hani's victory at Kolfarah being carved, picks up a broken brick, meets Manzat and plays a short tune for him.
**Evidence — Quote:** "معبد نارسینا در سینه سنگ، با دهانه رو به دره پدیدار شد."

### E06 — Nika confronts Hani in the temple; is arrested
**Chapter:** 1
**Scene:** Hani, Shoterouro, soldiers, and priests enter the temple while Nika plays; exchange about whether her song is "نیایش" (worship) or "صدای مردم" (the people's voice); a priest debates her; Hani orders her taken.
**Evidence — Quote:** "ـ او را ببرید. سربازها جلو آمدند."

### E07 — Hani reads the tally of Shileyti dead and breaks a tablet
**Chapter:** 2
**Scene:** Hani cannot sleep; reads a list of Shileyti's slain (20 leaders, 63 named, 12 women, one unnamed child) and breaks the tablet in anger/grief; Hohin's bed found empty.
**Evidence — Quote:** "فهرست کشتههای شیلهیتی: بیست سرکرده، شصتوسه نام، دوازده زن و کودکی بینام."

### E08 — Hohin secretly records the names of Shileyti's widows and fatherless children
**Chapter:** 2
**Evidence — Quote:** "هوهین آن شب، دور از کاخ، در گوشهای میان مردم نشسته بود. لوحی روی زانویش بود و با نوک انگشت، روی گِلِ نرم مینوشت. اسمها."

### E09 — Nika held bound in the cave below Kolfarah
**Chapter:** 2
**Scene:** Guards mock/watch her; she finds ancient scratched symbols on the cave wall ("قایق"، "سنگ"، "...در سنگ بگذار...") and adds her own drawing of a boat.

### E10 — Moraad's rescue party attacks the cave guards; Hish is killed
**Chapter:** 2
**Scene:** Moraad leads villagers from Takab, Bard-Boran, and other villages; a sentry is silenced with thrown stones; a guard's spear kills the elderly man Hish; Nika is freed.
**Evidence — Quote:** "همان لحظه، نیزه به سینه پیرمرد خورد."

### E11 — Manzat and the young soldier return the chang to Nika
**Chapter:** 2
**Evidence — Quote:** "مانْزات جلو دوید و چنگ را به دستش داد: ـ فکر کردم اگر چنگت بسوزد، آوازهایت هم میسوزد."

### E12 — Kourash's caravan travels to Golshor (Raju, the shepherd, Kourash introduced)
**Chapter:** 3
**Scene:** Cart breaks down in mud, is repaired with help from a shepherd and gapons; the group descends into Golshor at dusk.

### E13 — Discussions at the Golshor caravanserai (Ata, Selo, Halazh, Hohin present)
**Chapter:** 3
**Scene:** Talk of grain shares, water division customs in "سِند," Halazh brings a coded scroll from Lahson describing a village water-council; Hohin (unnamed to the reader until identified) listens quietly, is identified as Hani's wife by Kourash.

### E14 — Ayapir bazaar morning; Shoterouro and Hotran observe market and day laborers
**Chapter:** 4
**Scene:** Market activity, tax-seal stamping on jars, day laborers (نونتِلی) waiting for work, Chishpish's buffalo-hide storytelling to children about a falcon god.

### E15 — News of Nika's escape reaches the Ayapir bazaar
**Chapter:** 4
**Evidence — Quote:** "ـ نیکا گریخت!" — spreads through the market and then the fields; a farmhand: "اگر نیکا توانسته باشد..."

### E16 — Hani's cavalry rides out from Ayapir toward Zagros; news outruns them
**Chapter:** 4
**Evidence — Quote:** "پیش از آنکه نخستین سوار به گردنه سَراک برسد، در گُلِشور، شیلهیتی، دزپارت، سوسن و دهها آبادی دیگر مردم خبر را شنیده بودند."

### E17 — Spontaneous night gathering in Ayapir villages (半-mourning, half-celebration)
**Chapter:** 4
**Scene:** People come out of their homes unbidden; drums (دف) begin; a grieving old woman is first to step forward; a voice calls "داغدارها جلوتر بایستند."

### E18 — Hani orders the search of Shileyti "without unnecessary bloodshed"
**Chapter:** 4
**Evidence — Quote:** "شیلهیتی را بگردید... اما بیهوده خون نریزید."

### E19 — Hani finds his father's old tablet about a three-day closure of Golshor
**Chapter:** 4
**Evidence — Quote:** "«گُلِشور سه روز بسته شد. مردم در درهی دیگر گرد آمدند. فرمانِ بستن برداشته شد»."

### E20 — Hani reads Hohin's tablet about a widow searching for fodder in the rain
**Chapter:** 4
**Evidence — Quote:** "«هانی! امروز در گُلِشور زنی را دیدم که نوزادش را به پشت بسته بود و زیر باران دنبال علوفه میگشت. شوهرش در جنگ کشته شده بود»..."

### E21 — Kuru and Shoterouro depart Ayapir to search for Nika
**Chapter:** 5
**Scene:** They pass through ruined regions (Marga, Sadat, Shayvand, Ghariwal, etc.), post a public proclamation of reprisal at the spring of Mozram, encounter a woman searching burnt ashes for anything salvageable.

### E22 — Shoterouro and Kuru separate; Shoterouro returns to Ayapir
**Chapter:** 5
**Evidence — Quote:** "نه، کورو. من به آیاپیر برمیگردم... هانی باید بداند در زاگرس چه میگذرد."

### E23 — Nika, Moraad, and Manzat evade pursuers in the mountains (cave hiding, river-wading to hide tracks)
**Chapter:** 5
**Scene:** They hide in a bat-smelling cave from mounted searchers; later walk through a stream to erase footprints; reach the outskirts of Shileyti before dawn.

### E24 — Ani (Anieynoma) introduced at Shileyti, awake reading an old tablet
**Chapter:** 5
**Scene:** Chapter ends on a cliffhanger: a dog barks outside; footsteps heard on dry leaves.

### E25 — Golshor debates continue: Ata's hide-picture of ordinary laborers
**Chapter:** 6
**Scene:** Ata displays a hide depicting a toolmaker, a hide-sewer, wood-gathering children, asking why such people are absent from official carvings; a thief caught stealing walnuts is confronted gently by Ata ("هیچ دستی اینطور به دنیا نمیآید").

### E26 — Second hide unveiled: laborers under a whip, a man seated in shade
**Chapter:** 6
**Scene:** Jabro and Selo comment on unequal burden; a storm begins; a leak is caught in a clay bowl; conversation about water/land distribution as metaphor.

### E27 — Nighttime attack on Shileyti begins
**Chapter:** 7
**Scene:** Kuru present with local forces; a scribe records "یاغیان مقاومت کردند" before the fighting starts; Hani's cavalry attacks from both sides of the valley; the fodder barn is set ablaze.

### E28 — Nika is fatally wounded and dies
**Chapter:** 7
**Scene:** See character entry for full quote sequence — axe-handle blow, two arrows, dialogue with Ani, death.

### E29 — Moraad's grief and Nika's burial
**Chapter:** 7
**Scene:** Buried under an oak by the Shileyti spring with a marked broken brick instead of a headstone; Ani keeps Nika's final confession secret; Manzat initially declares "دیگر نمیزنیم. تا وقتی که نیکا برگردد" then secretly plays once at night.

### E30 — A grieving mother buries a single grain of wheat in the ashes of her burnt home
**Chapter:** 7 (closing scene)
**Evidence — Quote:** "زیر خاک، چیزی برق زد. یک دانه طالیی نسوخته و سالم گندم... دانه را در آن گذاشت و رویش را با خاک پوشاند."

### E31 — Five days after Nika's death: rebuilding begins at Shileyti amid ongoing uncertainty
**Chapter:** 8

### E32 — Messenger from Hani/Hohin arrives proposing a ceasefire
**Chapter:** 8
**Evidence — Quote:** "از زاپوالسیمی میآیم. هوهین پیام فرستاده... هانی آتشبس میخواهد."

### E33 — Traveler reports Halazh has been arrested; rumor that Hani did not order Nika's killing
**Chapter:** 8

### E34 — Hani and Hohin arrive at Shileyti in person; Kuru's protest and reconciliation with sword
**Chapter:** 8
**Scene:** See Hani and Kuru character entries.

### E35 — Hani declares the ceasefire publicly

### E36 — A grieving mother and a farmer whose home burned refuse reconciliation with Hani; crowd de-escalates

### E37 — Manzat raises the chang before Hani at Hohin's instruction; a marker-line is cut by the water as a memorial
**Chapter:** 8

### E38 — Shoterouro's journal entry the night of the ceasefire

### E39 — Three weeks after the ceasefire: villages act without waiting for royal orders
**Chapter:** 9
**Scene:** Reports that villages in Tanvash refuse the tax collector, villages in Zaras divide seized grain themselves, villages in Boluta'k lower Tirutir's flag.

### E40 — Nisiker presents Hani with three tablets of unpaid tax/unreturned soldiers
**Chapter:** 9
**Evidence — Quote:** "«خراج نرسید». «غله نرسید». «بیست سرباز، بازنگشتند». «پنج نفر، زره را زمین گذاشتند»."

### E41 — Hani refuses to reassert control by force; realizes "the order left our hands before it left here"
**Chapter:** 9

### E42 — Shoterouro's private journal-line: "sometimes decisions rise from the villages and reach the palace"
**Chapter:** 9

### E43 — Nisiker and the army commander press Hani to break the ceasefire; Hani refuses; the commander effectively threatens defection
**Chapter:** 9
**Evidence — Quote:** "اگر شاه از سپاهش استفاده نکند... سپاه هم شاه دیگری برای خود پیدا میکند."

### E44 — No decree is written that day; the order "goes silent" (خاموشی فرمان)
**Chapter:** 9 — Title event of the chapter.

### E45 — Nisiker relinquishes the treasury seal to Hani, declaring the treasury now belongs to whoever holds the army
**Chapter:** 9

### E46 — Hani departs the palace with Kourash's caravan
**Chapter:** 9
**Scene:** Takes up a hoe, sits beside a goat in the cart, says he is going "هر جا کاری باشد." Only rumor of his whereabouts follows.

### E47 — Shoterouro writes a closing aphorism and lays down his pen
**Chapter:** 9
**Evidence — Quote:** "بر سنگ، نامِ شاهان ماند؛ اما راه را دستهایی ساختند که نامی نداشتند."

### E48 — Three winters after the ceasefire: villagers gather at Khong-e-Azhdar
**Chapter:** 10
**Scene:** People converge from many named settlements; Shoterouro (now living at Golshor for three years) writes "امروز مردم آمدند" on a tablet.

### E49 — Communal labor and small comic incidents at the gathering (Hohin falls, is kicked by a cow)
**Chapter:** 10

### E50 — Discussion of old rock-relief kings and offerings; Mamuru's remark that bread only "becomes bread" when shared
**Chapter:** 10

### E51 — Manzat gives the chang to Ani; the wind plays the strings unassisted, taken as a "greeting" from Nika
**Chapter:** 10
**Evidence — Quote:** "سیمِ زیر، آرام لرزید. بعد سیمِ دیگر... هیچ دستی سیمها را ننواخته بود."

### E52 — Elderly woman calls for a discussion of water rights ("now that we've heard Nika's voice, it's water's turn")
**Chapter:** 10

### E53 — First bricks (representing willingness to try common governance) are laid by four volunteers; hesitation is shown but not forced
**Chapter:** 10

### E54 — A grieving man throws a stone at Shoterouro; Shoterouro does not resist or retaliate
**Chapter:** 10
**Scene:** Second man strikes his arm with a stick; Shoterouro's response: "سنگ، آدم را همیشه به زمین نمیاندازد؛ فقط جایِ آن میماند."

### E55 — Armed club-men (wearing Tirutir/Nahunte bone necklaces) attack the assembly with thrown stones
**Chapter:** 10
**Scene:** A woman and an old man are struck and injured; the crowd initially retreats then, led by Moraad and Jabro (with a hoe and a slab of stone), pushes back; the club-men flee.

### E56 — Villagers capture two of the fleeing club-men; Moraad and Jabro throw them into the river as retribution/humor; the crowd nearly turns violent before Mamuru intervenes
**Chapter:** 10
**Evidence — Quote:** "اگر امروز ما هم همین کار را بکنیم... فردا چه فرقی با آنها خواهیم داشت؟"

### E57 — Hohin and Ani treat the injured (a woman's bruised shoulder, an old man's leg)
**Chapter:** 10

### E58 — Kourash's cart arrives mid-aftermath; comic deflation of tension
**Chapter:** 10

### E59 — Evening of the same day: formal shura process begins with named committee items (water, barley, road, storehouse)
**Chapter:** 11
**Scene:** Ausfahrad moderates; Kourash's ear-cropped dog is jokingly "inducted"; debate over upstream/downstream water rights.

### E60 — Hotran objects to collective governance; is invited to contribute accounting skill but declines to vote
**Chapter:** 11

### E61 — First formal secret-ballot-style brick vote is held (bricks placed beside a "flowing water" mark or a "standing/still" mark)
**Chapter:** 11
**Scene:** Manzat casts first; the man who earlier stoned Shoterouro also votes (for the water/change side) without meeting anyone's eyes; Hotran again abstains.

### E62 — A royal envoy and soldiers arrive demanding to speak with "whoever has authority"; are rebuffed peacefully by the crowd
**Chapter:** 11
**Evidence — Quote:** "اگر با آب کار داری، با شورا حرف بزن... اختیارِ آب و زمین، اینجاست."

### E63 — Two priests remark privately that popular arbitration reduces the temple's share of judicial authority; the group decides the next assembly will be held at Eshkaft-e-Salman (closer to more villages, with water and orchards)
**Chapter:** 11 (closing scene)

**Chapter:** 12

### E67 — A woman proposes that anyone wanting water must also bring a shovel for canal-clearing (qanat maintenance); a rota system with knotted ropes is set up

### E69 — Rumor of old noble households reorganizing and unnamed men asking after specific people; a solitary rider from Shush inspects the assembly and rides off
**Chapter:** 12

### E70 — Evening campfire: a teenage girl hesitantly plays Nika's chang for the first time since Nika's death, using a lullaby she knows; it becomes accepted as "چنگِ میدان" (the chang of the assembly) rather than "Nika's chang"

### E71 — Village elder teaches children the name "هَلْتَمتی" and regional history/identity

### E72 — Napahun dances; communal celebration

### E75 — Traveler from Shush reports old noble households regrouping and unnamed men asking after specific individuals
**Chapter:** 13
**Evidence — Quote:** "بعضی خاندانهای قدیمی دوباره جمع شدهاند... مردانی را هم دیدهام که نه بازرگان بودند، نه سرباز؛ فقط میپرسیدند مردم کجا دور هم جمع میشوند."

### E76 — Kuru is fatally shot by an unseen archer near the mountain pass as the caravan departs
**Chapter:** 13
**Scene:** See Kuru's character entry for full quote sequence; his epitaph is composed collectively and written by Shoterouro; his brick and Manzat's oak-leaf boat are set adrift together in the irrigation ditch.

### E77 — Caravan continues onward; the novel ends on the road, with Manzat running after the departing boat/brick
**Chapter:** 13 (final lines)
**Evidence — Quote:** "دیگر نه قایق پیدا بود، نه خشت. فقط آب میگذشت. او دوید."
