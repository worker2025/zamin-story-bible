# Film Bible: Character Continuity

## Rule
Physical continuity must respect only what is documented per character in
V10_VisualDesign.md and each Characters/ card. Where no visual detail is
given, continuity is a production decision, not canon, and must remain
consistent only within that production's own invented design — not
presented as sourced from the novel.

## Key Continuity Facts (explicitly stated, must not be contradicted)

- Nika: rope-wounded wrists and dark dried blood on forearms during
  captivity (Chapters 2 onward until rescue); torn, dusty shirt during
  captivity. Dies in Chapter 7 — struck by axe-handle to the shoulder,
  then two arrows (shoulder, chest). Must not appear after Chapter 7
  except in flashback/memory, per Story Bible.
- Hani: wears a crown/tāj; smudges lamp-soot onto its corner at one
  point (Chapter 1). Departs the palace in Chapter 9 and is not seen again
  on-page; any appearance after Chapter 9 beyond rumor is NOT canon.
- Kuru: hands marked from stonecutting; sword returned to him by Hani
  after he throws it down (Chapter 8) — he must be shown carrying it again
  post-Chapter 8. Dies in Chapter 13 (arrow to the chest, old cotton
  shirt/"چلوار کهنه").
- Hish: dies in Chapter 2; must not appear afterward except in others'
  memory/reference (e.g., his sickle reappearing in Chapter 10, carried by
  an unnamed woman).
- Hohin: appears without armor/helmet specifically at the Chapter 8
  ceasefire scene; simple dress with fine sleeve-fabric and silver pin
  noted at Golshor (Chapter 3).
