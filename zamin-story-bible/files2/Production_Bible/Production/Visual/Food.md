# Visual Bible: Food

## Stated Food References

- Bread (نان) — baked by Mamuru at Kohbad's tanoor (barley/acorn-flour bread implied by his baker role); given to a barefoot child; distributed to children at Khong-e-Azhdar; bread held to the fire's flame by Selo per "his father's custom" at the Eshkaft-e-Salman campfire. (V02_Characters.md, V07_Culture.md)
- Fava-bean stew (باقله) — sold by Ebram-Baghleh near the councils. (V02_Characters.md)
- Doogh (دوغ) — a yogurt-based drink, churned using the malar (تripod). (V05_Objects.md)
- Fine animal oil (روغنِ خوش) — part of the estate's levy demand, stored in a khig. (V05_Objects.md)
- Wheat/grain (گندم) — central agricultural staple; referenced throughout in tax, levy, and rebuilding contexts. (V03_Events.md)
- Wool-thread (پشموخُمه) is a levy item but not a food. (Listed here only to distinguish from food items in the same levy list.) (V05_Objects.md)
