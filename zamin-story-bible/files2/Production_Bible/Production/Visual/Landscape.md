# Visual Bible: Landscape

## Stated Landscape Elements

- Ayapir: wide plain among mountains; surrounding peaks named مُنْگَشت (southeast, casts shadow), الهَک و چیوه (watch over passes); slope vegetation of oak, sidr/jujube, wild pistachio, greening in spring; a seasonal, winding stream. (Source: V04_Locations.md)
- Golshor: reached via a mountain pass; overlooking peak named بَلیطبلند. (V04_Locations.md)
- Shileyti: mountain region; spring beside an old oak; cliffs. (V04_Locations.md)
- Khong-e-Azhdar: described as dark, silent rock outcroppings ("تیره و خاموش"). (V04_Locations.md)
- Eshkaft-e-Salman: orchards, spring, qanats. (V04_Locations.md)
- General Zagros mountain terrain crossed during the search for Nika (Chapter 5): named passes/valleys (Baajol, Gaveileh, Garmet, Aukhigoun, Avand, Mozram, Ardal, Kori) — no further terrain description given beyond the names. (V04_Locations.md)
