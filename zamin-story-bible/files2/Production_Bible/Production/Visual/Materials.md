# Visual Bible: Materials

## Stated Materials (by category)

### Construction
- Stone (سنگ) — palace (Zapol-e-Simi), temple sites (Kolfarah, Eshkaft-e-Salman, Khong-e-Azhdar), Golshor stable walls. (V04_Locations.md)
- Clay/mud (گِل) — Kohbad houses, Golshor roofs, clay tablets, bricks. (V04_Locations.md, V05_Objects.md)
- Wood — implied for roof structure/beams (not detailed), spade handles, cradles (Tahdeh), spinning tools (Pahreh), tripods (Malar). (V05_Objects.md)

### Costume/Textile
- Leather/hide (پوست) — khig, mashk, buffalo-hide displays. (V05_Objects.md)
- Wool (پشم) — pashm-o-khomeh (spun wool thread). (V05_Objects.md)
- Cotton cloth (چلوار) — Kuru's shirt at death. (V10_VisualDesign.md)

### Weapons/Tools
- Wood — spears (some explicitly "چوبی," wooden), shepherd's poles, clubs (گرزهای چوبی), Moraad's wooden spear. (V02_Characters.md, V10_VisualDesign.md)
- Metal (implied, not always specified) — swords (شمشیر), sickles (داس), adzes/chisels (تیشه); metal type UNKNOWN.
- Stone — throwable stones (بَرْد و کُچِک), grinding stones (دستاس), mortar (used by Ani). (V05_Objects.md)

## UNKNOWN
- Specific metal types (bronze, iron, etc.) are not stated anywhere in the Story Bible. This is a notable gap: the Story Bible does not specify weapon/tool metallurgy.
