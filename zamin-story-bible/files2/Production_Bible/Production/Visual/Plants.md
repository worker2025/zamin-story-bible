# Visual Bible: Plants

## Stated Plant References

- Oak (بلوط) — recurring throughout: oak-leaf boat motif; old oak at Shileyti under which Nika is buried; oak groves in the Zagros mountains generally. (V04_Locations.md, V05_Objects.md)
- Sidr / jujube (کُنار) — named among slope vegetation near Ayapir. (V04_Locations.md)
- Wild pistachio (بنه) — named among slope vegetation near Ayapir. (V04_Locations.md)
- Sukut (سُکُتْ) — a plant used to make hand-brooms; noted at the Ayapir bazaar's game area ("کلدوزخ"). (V04_Locations.md, V05_Objects.md footnote reference)
- Barley (جو) — referenced as a shura agenda item ("جو" among آب، جو، راه، انبار). (V07_Culture.md)
- Wheat (گندم) — a golden grain buried in the ashes by a grieving mother (Chapter 7); referenced generally in tax/grain contexts. (V03_Events.md E30)
- Thyme (dried) — present among Ani's herbs at Shileyti. (V02_Characters.md, per Ani's introduction)
- Walnuts — a thief is caught stealing walnuts at Golshor. (V03_Events.md E25)
